# Organizational Optimization System

## Purpose
Detect and prioritize organization-wide inefficiencies, recurring bottlenecks, and cross-team coordination friction that degrade SLA and throughput goals.

## Implemented Diagnostics
- bottleneck pressure detection
- recurring friction scoring
- intent-to-objective drift detection
- strategic simulation prioritization for optimization interventions

## Optimization Pattern
1. Detect high-impact recurring friction.
2. Map friction to affected objectives.
3. Evaluate candidate simulation strategies.
4. Generate explainable optimization recommendations.
5. Track recommendation lineage in cognitive memory.

## Example Guidance Pattern
"Recommend temporary reconciliation pod restructuring during peak filing periods due to projected congestion and SLA impact."

## Traceability
- optimization decisions are stored as cognitive memory entries
- governance decisions are retained in recommendation audit records
- alignment and conflict scores can be surfaced to executives
