Date: 2026-06-04

Purpose: evaluate whether CAATH is ready to move from internal pilot operations to first external pilot customer deployment.

## Executive Summary

CAATH is ready to be used internally as an operational pilot platform. The current priority is not new architecture; it is proof of repeated daily execution across client, task, compliance, document, notification, and GST workflows.

GST Intelligence is conditionally certified for controlled pilot deployment using persisted GST operational data. It must not be positioned as fully certified raw GST file ingestion yet.

## Pilot Exit Criteria

CAATH should advance to first external pilot only when:

1. Zero open P0 bugs.
2. No unresolved P1 issue blocks task, client, compliance, GST persisted-data analysis, or document workflows.
3. Five internal users complete at least five working days in CAATH.
4. At least 50 real tasks are created and 35 completed.
5. At least 20 clients are created or validated.
6. GST pilot flow is executed with clear persisted-data evidence.
7. Every observed confusion point has an owner and decision.

## Executive Decision

Current decision: **Proceed with internal pilot operations. Do not yet onboard external pilot customer until internal evidence is collected and P0/P1 blockers are closed.**
