# Enterprise Scenario Modeling

## Scenario Types
- workflow_surge
- task_routing
- escalation_cascade
- staff_reassignment
- sla_pressure
- capacity_stress
- compliance_risk

## Scenario Inputs
- incoming workflow volume
- average handle time
- escalation probability
- reassignment efficiency gain
- staff availability delta

## What-if Analysis
The digital twin supports:
- peak filing-period stress tests
- staffing alternatives and impact comparison
- reassignment strategy outcomes
- congestion mitigation plans
- compliance risk drift forecasting

## Cross-Domain Federation
Scenario modeling consumes federated operational signals from:
- workflow and queue events
- escalation and risk signals
- predictive intelligence context
- operational memory and historical traces

## Output
Explainable simulation artifacts for executive and operations planning, not production automation.
