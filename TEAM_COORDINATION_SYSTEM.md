# Team Coordination System

The team coordination system gives operators shared awareness without forcing them into a generic chat surface.

## Team Surfaces

- Operator Presence.
- Team Workload Board.
- Operational Handoff.
- Shared Operational Timeline.
- Contextual Operational Communication.
- Shared Intelligence.
- Collaborative Notifications.
- Runtime-Safe Collaboration.
- Cross-Domain Federation.

## Workspace Integration

The realtime workspace includes a compact Collaborative Operations snapshot showing:

- Active operators.
- Pending handoffs.
- Collaboration signals.
- Shortcuts to the full Team Coordination workspace.

## Fast Access

- `Ctrl+K`: Search collaboration commands.
- `Alt+G`: Open Team Coordination.
- `Alt+H`: Create Workflow Handoff.
