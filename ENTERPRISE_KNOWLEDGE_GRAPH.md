# Enterprise Knowledge Graph

The enterprise knowledge graph maps operational relationships across workflows, GST intelligence, clients, escalations, collaboration, governance, and playbooks.

## Current Graph Nodes

- GST Intelligence.
- Resolution Memory.
- Notice Escalations.
- Approval Governance.
- Collaborative Notes.
- Knowledge Governance.
- Operational Playbooks.
- Client Operational Patterns.

## Goal

The graph gives operators a way to understand which operational memories, playbooks, workflows, and governance controls influence each other.

## Implementation

Graph data is modeled in `src/domains/organizational-memory/organizationalMemoryCore.ts` and rendered in `LearningDashboard.tsx`.
