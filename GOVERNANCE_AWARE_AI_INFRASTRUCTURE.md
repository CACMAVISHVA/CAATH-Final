# Governance-Aware AI Infrastructure

CAATH AI assistance is designed to remain constrained by governance, permissions and auditability.

## Governance Controls

- Permission-federated assistance
- Recommendation auditability
- Human-controlled execution
- Approval-threshold awareness
- Sensitive workflow routing

## Safeguards

- Workflow action validation prevents hallucinated route execution.
- Recommendation rhythm control prevents excessive AI interruptions.
- Suggestion reconciliation detects conflicting AI guidance across domains.
- Governance gate enforcement routes sensitive items through approval and governance surfaces.

## Enterprise Trust

AI recommendations must be explainable before they become useful. CAATH therefore displays reasoning, source workflows, confidence, permission scope and governance rationale directly inside the operational UI.
