# Governed Collaboration System

Collaboration in CAATH is permission-aware and workflow-attached.

## Governance Behaviors

- Restricted workflows expose only appropriate context.
- Handoffs preserve ownership and continuity.
- Mentions and comments remain attached to workflow context.
- Collaboration activity is traceable in governance timelines.
- Escalation ownership chains are visible.
- AI recommendations retain rationale and operator attribution.

## Runtime Safety

The system prevents collaboration overload through:

- Batched notifications.
- Restricted visibility surfaces.
- Governance event throttling.
- Permission synchronization controls.

## Result

Teams can coordinate quickly while preserving compliance-grade operational trust.
