# Operational Auditability Infrastructure

CAATH governance surfaces expose traceability across workflow actions, collaboration, AI recommendations, approvals, overrides, and escalations.

## Audit Surfaces

- Governance Timeline.
- Auditability Infrastructure.
- Workflow Approval Infrastructure.
- Permission Intelligence.
- Accountability Surface.

## Audit Coverage

Audit events include:

- Permission changes.
- Governance actions.
- Approval history.
- Workflow overrides.
- Operational escalations.
- Access transitions.
- AI recommendation audit history.
- Collaboration traceability.

## Design Principle

Auditability is visible in the operational workspace and governance dashboard instead of being hidden in passive logs.
