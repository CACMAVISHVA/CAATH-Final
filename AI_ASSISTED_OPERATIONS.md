# AI Assisted Operations

## Implemented Flow
- `AIAssistedOperationsOrchestrator` pulls operational intelligence signals.
- Prompts pass through runtime AI governance authorization before use.
- Returned recommendations provide severity, reason, and suggested action.

## Use Cases
- Overdue workflow detection
- Approval pressure warnings
- Operational bottleneck guidance
- Workload risk suggestions

## Safety Guarantees
- AI calls are policy-evaluated by runtime AI governance.
- AI usage is correlated and audit-linked.
