# Operational Intelligence Fabric

## Purpose
CAATH now includes a unified enterprise operational intelligence fabric to coordinate GST intelligence, AI orchestration, predictive systems, workflows, operational memory, dashboards, notifications, and task systems through one federated operational layer.

## Core Domain
- Path: `src/domains/operational-fabric/`
- Entry orchestrator: `OperationalIntelligenceFabricOrchestrator`
- Federation model: modular, loosely coupled, event-driven, governance-aware

## Fabric Capabilities
- Cross-domain event federation through `CrossDomainIntelligenceBus`
- Operational context federation via `OperationalContextEngine`
- Enterprise memory unification via `EnterpriseOperationalMemoryFabric`
- Cross-domain risk and workflow correlation through `CrossDomainCorrelationEngine`
- Unified timeline assembly through `UnifiedOperationalTimeline`
- Enterprise graph intelligence through `EnterpriseOperationalGraph`
- AI coordination and summaries via `AIEnterpriseCoordinationLayer`
- Federated search via `OperationalIntelligenceSearchFabric`
- Runtime-safe governance and routing via `RuntimeFederationGovernance`
- Storm-safe runtime controls via `PerformanceAwareFederationRuntime`

## Enterprise Outcome
- Single operational intelligence layer
- Shared context propagation across domains
- Unified executive visibility with domain heatmaps and correlated risk
- AI-assisted operational coordination at enterprise scope
