# Operational Throughput Engine

## Purpose
The throughput engine evaluates operational acceleration potential under governance-safe coordination plans.

## Metrics
- throughput score
- queue optimization score
- velocity balance score
- congestion risk

## Inputs
- queue pressure index
- throughput index
- rebalance net impact
- SLA risk reduction signals

## Output Behavior
- identifies congestion risk zones
- recommends queue-safe routing posture
- surfaces when redistribution should be throttled or continued

## Reliability
- bounded scoring
- explainable recommendation generation
- designed for realtime dashboard overlays
