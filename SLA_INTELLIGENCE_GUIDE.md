# SLA Intelligence Guide

Date: 2026-05-25

## Scope
SLA intelligence across workflow/task execution with proactive breach prevention.

## SLA Indicators
- urgency score
- breach probability
- escalation score
- high-risk and medium-risk counts

## Operational Usage
- prioritize high-risk tasks from AI queue
- trigger governed escalation for critical items
- send reminders for near-breach items
- track SLA risk movement via telemetry and unified stream

## Governance
SLA guidance is explainable and action-driven, but human-approved before critical irreversible changes.
