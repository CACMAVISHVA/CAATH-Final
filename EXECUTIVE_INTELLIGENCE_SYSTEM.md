# Executive Intelligence System

Date: 2026-05-25

## Objective
Provide strategic operational oversight for leadership with real-time execution risk visibility.

## Executive Signals
- active clients
- pending workloads
- escalation alerts
- workflow health
- SLA high-risk/medium-risk counts
- AI queue pressure

## Intelligence Sources
- dashboard metrics service
- operational health summary
- AI operations center snapshot
- AI workflow optimization recommendations

## Outcome
Executive teams get a single operational control narrative instead of disconnected analytics.
