# Operational AI Workflows

Date: 2026-05-25

## Core AI Workflow Capabilities
- Overdue workflow recommendations
- Workload balancing hints
- Compliance risk recommendations
- Task prioritization queue generation
- Operational search command suggestions
- Notification-driven nudges

## Workflow Pattern
1. Collect operational signals.
2. Authorize AI operation with governance context.
3. Build recommendations and risk-prioritized actions.
4. Surface outputs in dashboard cards and nudges.
5. Persist execution traces through existing telemetry/runtime.
