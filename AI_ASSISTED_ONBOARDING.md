# AI Assisted Onboarding

Date: 2026-05-25

## Purpose
Use contextual guidance to accelerate user activation without overload.

## Implemented Pattern
- Role-specific AI activation hints in onboarding modal.
- Suggestions tied to operational next-best actions.
- Guidance remains safe, advisory, and non-destructive.

## Example Guidance
- SuperAdmin: run first GST intelligence scan.
- Admin: configure approvals and escalation notifications.
- Staff: start task lane and filing checks.
- Client: upload first document and track compliance.

## Governance Expectations
- AI hints do not auto-execute workflows.
- Operators approve and trigger actions explicitly.
- Activation events are telemetry-traceable.
