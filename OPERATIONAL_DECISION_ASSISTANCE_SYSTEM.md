# Operational Decision Assistance System

The decision assistance system helps operators move faster without losing governance context. It prioritizes actionable workflow guidance over passive insight display.

## Assistance Types

- SLA intervention guidance
- Next-best-action recommendations
- Escalation assistance
- Queue prioritization
- Risk explanations
- Governance recommendations
- Executive briefings

## Recommendation Contract

Each recommendation carries:

- operational summary
- recommended next action
- target route
- confidence score
- source workflows
- context lineage
- governance rationale
- permission scope
- audit trail
- operational impact

## Operator Behavior

Operators can open context, apply guidance or dismiss recommendations. Apply and dismiss actions are wired through the unified operational action executor, giving CAATH feedback states, cooldown protection, telemetry and undo where appropriate.
