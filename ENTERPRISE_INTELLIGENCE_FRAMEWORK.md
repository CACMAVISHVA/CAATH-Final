# CAATH Enterprise Intelligence Framework

The enterprise intelligence framework keeps CAATH analytics connected to real operations.

## Intelligence Layers

- Realtime telemetry.
- Workflow analytics.
- Predictive operations.
- Executive KPIs.
- AI-assisted recommendations.
- Operational intelligence memory.

## Memory Strategy

Analytics retains historical trend memory for throughput, SLA risk and anomaly counts. This supports longitudinal visibility and future model recalibration.

## Future Extensibility

The architecture is designed for ML model federation, external BI integrations, enterprise forecasting engines, financial intelligence and AI operational copilots.
