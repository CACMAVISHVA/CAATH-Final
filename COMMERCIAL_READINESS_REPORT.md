# Commercial Readiness Report

Date: 2026-06-04

## Executive Assessment

CAATH is approaching pilot readiness, not broad commercial readiness. The product now has a cleaner shell, green lint/build, support ticket helpers, and a plausible onboarding path. The remaining commercialization risk is operational proof: real tenants, real workflows, support SLAs, and billing enforcement must be validated before taking the first paying customer beyond pilot.

## Readiness Scores

| Area | Score | Status | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Deployment readiness | 7.4 | Partial | Build/lint pass; staging E2E and RLS tests remain |
| Support readiness | 7.2 | Partial | Ticket/feedback/error/escalation helpers exist; support UI/SLA reporting needs validation |
| Onboarding readiness | 7.5 | Partial | Playbook and first-run guide exist; real firm dry run needed |
| Operational readiness | 7.0 | Partial | Core workflows exist; non-GST compliance and analytics lineage need work |
| Customer readiness | 6.8 | Partial | Pilot scope is viable; paid readiness requires real proof of workflow completion |

Overall commercial readiness: 7.2 / 10

## Ready For

- Internal dry run.
- Controlled pilot with one friendly CA firm.
- Limited workflow rollout focused on clients, tasks, documents, GST, notices, approvals.

## Not Ready For

- Broad public launch.
- Multi-firm scaled onboarding.
- Usage-based billing without metering validation.
- Positioning AI/autonomous operations as core commercial promise.

## Commercial Gate

Move from pilot to paid when:

- P0 launch punch list is closed.
- Firm completes 30 days of core workflows.
- Support load is manageable.
- Billing/plan terms are validated.
- Customer confirms ROI.
