# Executive Intelligence Fabric

## Executive Layer
- Domain path: `src/domains/executive-fabric-dashboard/`
- Orchestrator: `ExecutiveFabricDashboardOrchestrator`
- Input: unified snapshot from operational fabric orchestrator

## Executive Outputs
- Cross-domain KPI surface
- Enterprise operational heatmap by domain intensity
- Top correlated risk signals
- AI-generated enterprise summary
- Predictive operational guidance

## Design Principles
- Executive visibility is derived from federated operational truth
- No separate dashboard logic forks from operational runtime
- Risk, context, and prediction are rendered from shared fabric state

## Strategic Value
- Faster executive decision cycles
- Cohesive cross-domain governance posture
- Reduced blind spots between compliance, workflow, and AI operations
