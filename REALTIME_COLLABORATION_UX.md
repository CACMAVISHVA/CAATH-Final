# Realtime Collaboration UX

Realtime collaboration in CAATH is operationally scoped.

## UX Patterns

- Presence indicators show who is active, reviewing, idle, or in handoff.
- Occupancy shows which workspace or workflow an operator is touching.
- Timeline events combine mentions, handoffs, escalation updates, approvals, and shared insights.
- Communication is attached to workflow context.
- Alerts are batched to prevent collaboration overload.

## Safety Controls

- Batched notifications.
- Muted signal memory.
- Sync controls for workspace churn.
- Collaboration surfaces remain panel-based and workflow-specific.

## Result

Operators get live coordination visibility without notification floods or detached chat rooms.
