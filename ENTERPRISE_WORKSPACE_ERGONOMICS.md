# Enterprise Workspace Ergonomics

The workspace is optimized for reduced clicks and keyboard-driven execution.

## Keyboard Entry Points

- `Ctrl+K`: Universal search and command palette.
- `Alt+X`: Open Realtime Workspace.
- `Alt+N`: Create task.
- `Alt+A`: Open approvals.
- `Alt+Q`: Quick approve.
- `Alt+R`: Bulk resolve.
- `Alt+W`: Trigger workflow.

## Rapid Actions

- New task.
- Quick approve.
- Bulk resolve.
- Open operational inbox.
- Assign work.
- Open GST context.
- Open AI queue.

## Ergonomic Patterns

- Dock rail for panel switching.
- Workspace tabs for active operational contexts.
- Inline action buttons in workflow surfaces.
- Live sync and optimistic action indicators.
- Compact density for repeated operational work.
