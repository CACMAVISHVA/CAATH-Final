# Autonomous Coordination Architecture

## Mission
CAATH now includes a governed autonomous coordination layer that optimizes workload flow without bypassing human governance or coupling into execution runtime.

- `src/domains/coordination-dashboard/`

- Coordination orchestrator
- Intelligent workload rebalancer
- Adaptive workflow router

- Realtime throughput optimization engine
- AI coordination intelligence
- Coordination timeline intelligence

- Human-approval signaling for high-risk plans
- Throttled coordination windows to avoid oscillation
- Decoupled recommendation and execution boundaries
