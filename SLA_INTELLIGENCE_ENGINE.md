# SLA Intelligence Engine

Date: 2026-05-25

## Purpose
Predict deadline and breach risk early to drive proactive escalation and reassignment actions.

## SLA Model
- Uses deadline proximity + task urgency + workflow state risk.
- Produces high-risk and medium-risk counts.
- Generates operational summary for queue and dashboards.

## Operational Use
- Highlight top SLA-risk workflows in AI queue.
- Recommend escalation or immediate owner assignment.
- Feed risk cues into AI notification nudges and control center snapshots.

## Governance
- Explanations are attached to each queue item.
- No automatic irreversible actions; operator confirms workflow changes.
