# Executive Decision Engine

The executive decision engine provides AI-assisted strategic operational guidance, not autonomous execution commands.

## Inputs
- objective hierarchy with status
- workload and friction signals
- operational intent confidence signals
- simulation scenarios for optimization pathways

## Decision Outputs
- strategic recommendations with explicit priority
- conflict-aware intervention guidance
- predicted operational outcomes
- organizational impact statements
- confidence-scored explainability payloads

## Governance Guarantees
- every recommendation receives a governance audit record
- policy traces are attached for reviewability
- low-confidence outputs are marked for executive review

## Design Principles
- explainability first
- decision support over auto-execution
- enterprise-safe recommendation load limits
- auditable recommendation lifecycle
