# CAATH Executive Analytics System

Executive analytics gives firm leadership a concise operational picture with traceable context.

## KPI Framework

- Operational health.
- Organization efficiency.
- Governance maturity.
- Automation impact.
- Ecosystem reliability.

## Executive Surface Rules

- KPIs must show trend, score and context.
- Health metrics should be tied to operational domains.
- Recommendations should include confidence and expected impact.
- Intelligence should remain actionable, not decorative.

## Roadmap

Future executive analytics can add financial intelligence, external BI exports, board-level summaries and AI-generated executive review packs.
