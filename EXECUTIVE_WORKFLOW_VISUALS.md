# CAATH Executive Workflow Visuals

Executive workflow visuals help CAATH feel like a premium operational OS rather than a noisy dashboard.

## Workflow Focus

- The live workflow area should be the clearest and most dominant region.
- SLA and risk indicators should stay visible but not visually overpower task content.
- Action rows should provide one clear primary action with quieter alternatives.
- Context panels should support the decision without pulling focus away from execution.

## Executive Surfaces

- KPI, risk, workload, SLA, governance, and learning panels should use calm metric cards.
- Metrics should be readable at a glance, with softer labels and restrained color.
- Use grouped whitespace to separate executive themes instead of heavy separators.

## Premium Polish

- Hover states should be smooth, subtle, and predictable.
- Active states should feel selected, not alarmed.
- Motion should reinforce orientation and continuity, not spectacle.
- The overall identity should reference Linear, Attio, Arc, Stripe, and SAP Fiori discipline while preserving CAATH's AI-native operational character.
