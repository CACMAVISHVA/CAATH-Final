# CAATH Predictive Operational Intelligence

Predictive intelligence gives operators forward-looking visibility into operational risk.

## Current Predictions

- SLA breach prediction.
- Queue overload forecasting.
- Escalation prediction.
- Integration-driven operational anomaly context.
- Operator capacity and workflow pressure signals.

## Explanation Contract

Every prediction includes:

- Prediction statement.
- Risk level.
- Confidence score.
- Rationale.
- Source workflows.
- Trace lineage.

## Safety

Predictions guide operators and command surfaces. They should not directly execute workflow mutation without governance and action-system controls.
