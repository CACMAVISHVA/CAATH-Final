# CAATH Governed Automation Infrastructure

Governed automation ensures autonomy remains safe, auditable and override-capable.

## Governance Controls

- Role-aware policies.
- Approval thresholds.
- Risk scoring.
- Throttle windows.
- Override availability.
- Execution lineage.

## Approval Logic

Low-risk automation may be auto-approved when confidence clears policy thresholds. Medium and high-risk automation require stronger policy checks, and high-risk automation remains approval-gated.

## Runtime Safety

Automation plans can be throttled to prevent operational storms. Throttling is visible in runtime health metrics and traceable through the automation timeline.
