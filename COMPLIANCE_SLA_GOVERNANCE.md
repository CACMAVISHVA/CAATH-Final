# Compliance SLA Governance

## SLA Intelligence
For each compliance issue:
- SLA deadline timestamp
- aging hours
- urgency score
- breach risk classification

## Operational Triggers
- High urgency raises escalation priority
- Breach-likely issues are surfaced in executive view
- Aging trends drive remediation sequencing

## Governance Guarantees
- SLA signals are explainable and issue-linked
- Escalation logic remains role-aware
- Traceability preserved via issue and timeline IDs
