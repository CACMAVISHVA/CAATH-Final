# AI-Native Operational Workspace

The AI-native workspace makes assistance contextual, calm and operationally relevant.

## UX Standards

- No generic chat widget as the primary surface.
- Recommendations appear beside workflow context.
- Executive summaries are concise and traceable.
- AI actions route to real CAATH surfaces.
- Permission-gated actions remain visible but constrained.
- Guidance is non-intrusive and does not disrupt focus mode.

## Workspace Surfaces

- AI Copilot command center
- Workflow-aware recommendation cards
- Executive decision assistance panels
- Governance-aware runtime policy panels
- AI activity feed
- Confidence and priority indicators

## Future Direction

Future copilots should remain attached to operational context: GST, governance, integrations, collaboration, organizational memory, analytics and workflow execution. AI should reduce decision friction while preserving operator control.
